Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 1, 2025

Introduction

ChatGPT Collapsible Turns ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles data.

Data Collection

What We Collect

  • Extension settings (on/off state, auto-collapse preferences)
  • Message collapse/expand states (stored as anonymous IDs)

What We DON'T Collect

  • Chat conversation content
  • Personal information
  • Browsing history
  • Any data outside of ChatGPT.com

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using:

  • Chrome's Sync Storage - For settings that sync across your devices
  • Session Storage - For temporary collapse states during your session
Important: No data is ever transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Data Usage

Collected data is used solely to:

  • Remember your extension preferences
  • Maintain your message collapse/expand states
  • Provide a consistent user experience

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data. Everything stays local to your browser.

Third-Party Access

The Extension does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms.

User Rights

You can:

  • Clear all extension data by disabling the extension
  • Clear session data by closing your browser tab
  • Remove all data by uninstalling the extension

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the Extension constitutes acceptance of any changes.
